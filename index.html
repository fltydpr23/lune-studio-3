How We Work

Four moves.
One direction.

01
Understand
Before anything is designed, we map the system. Revenue bottlenecks, customer psychology, competitive gaps. The brief is never only aesthetic.
We run a structured discovery to understand what's actually happening in your business — not just what you think the problem is. The real constraint is rarely what the client names first.
02
Architect
Information architecture, conversion flows, component systems. We build the skeleton before the skin — so everything underneath holds.
Wireframes, user flows, and content hierarchy before a single colour is chosen. Structure determines outcomes. Beauty is the final layer, not the first.
03
Build
Clean code, fast load times, pixel-level fidelity. We write production-ready work. No handoffs to mediocre developers. We are the developer.
Design and engineering are the same team. This eliminates the gap between what was designed and what ships — which is where most agencies lose quality.
04
Compound
Launch is day one, not the finish line. We track, iterate, and optimise until the numbers move the way they should.
Analytics, heatmaps, and conversion data reviewed at 7, 14, and 30 days post-launch. The work isn't done until the result is real.

Privacy Policy
Lune Studio — How we handle your data

1. Who we are

Lune Studio ("we", "our", "us") is a boutique design and engineering studio registered and operating in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. We can be contacted through our website inquiry form or directly via email. This Privacy Policy applies to our website at lunestudio.in and all services we provide.

2. What information we collect

We collect information only when you choose to provide it — typically when you contact us through our website, email, or WhatsApp. This includes:

  • Your name and email address (when you reach out to us)
  • Business name and project description (when you submit an enquiry)
  • Phone number or WhatsApp (if you choose to share it)
  • Technical data automatically collected: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited — via Google Analytics

We do not collect payment information directly. Any payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways that operate under their own privacy policies.

3. How we use your information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Respond to project enquiries and communications
  • Provide the services you have engaged us to deliver
  • Send project-related updates, invoices, and documentation
  • Improve our website and understand how visitors use it (via analytics)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third party. Ever.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, our legal basis for processing your data is: (a) your consent where explicitly given; (b) performance of a contract when you engage our services; (c) our legitimate interests in operating a business, which we assess do not override your rights.

5. Data retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Client project data is retained for a minimum of 3 years for contractual and legal purposes. You may request deletion at any time by writing to us.

6. Third-party services

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand visitor behaviour. Google Analytics collects anonymised data using cookies. We also use Google Workspace for email and document management. These third parties operate under their own privacy policies and data protection standards.

7. Your rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; object to processing; and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the bottom of this document will reflect any changes. Continued use of our website following changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please reach out to us or write to: Lune Studio, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.

Cookies Policy
Lune Studio — How and why we use cookies

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and provide a better experience on return visits. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

2. How we use cookies

Lune Studio uses a minimal set of cookies. We don't use tracking cookies for advertising or sell cookie data to third parties. Our cookies fall into the following categories:

2a. Strictly necessary cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They enable core functionality such as security and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies as the site cannot function without them.

  • lune_cookie_consent — Stores your cookie consent preference. Duration: 1 year.
  • lune_session — Maintains basic session state. Duration: Session (deleted when you close your browser).

2b. Analytics cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website — which pages are most visited, how long visitors stay, and where they come from. This helps us improve the experience. We use Google Analytics for this purpose.

  • _ga — Google Analytics. Distinguishes users. Duration: 2 years.
  • _ga_* — Google Analytics. Maintains session state. Duration: 1 year.
  • _gid — Google Analytics. Distinguishes users for 24 hours. Duration: 24 hours.

Google Analytics data is anonymised and aggregated. We do not collect personally identifiable information through analytics cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics across all websites using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

3. Cookies we do NOT use

We want to be direct: we do not use advertising cookies, remarketing cookies, social media tracking pixels, or any cookies that track you across other websites. Our website is a clean, non-intrusive experience.

4. Managing your cookie preferences

When you first visit our website, you'll be presented with a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept analytics cookies or decline them — strictly necessary cookies are always active. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

You can also manage cookies directly in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

5. Third-party cookies

If we embed third-party content (such as maps or video), those providers may set their own cookies. We have no control over these third-party cookies. Please refer to the relevant third party's cookie policy for details.

6. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. Please check this page periodically for updates.

7. Contact

Questions about our use of cookies? Please reach out to us.
